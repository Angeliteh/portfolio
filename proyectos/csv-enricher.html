The Problem

AI is powerful. Using it on spreadsheets is painful.

Professionals in HR, sales, finance, and marketing deal with hundreds or thousands of rows of unstructured CSV data every week. The manual process — reading each row, applying judgment, writing a result — is slow, inconsistent, and impossible to scale.

Existing solutions either require technical prompt engineering skills most users don't have, or are so generic they can't adapt to a specific company's criteria and workflows. The gap between "I have this data" and "I need this intelligence applied to it" was enormous.

The Solution

Business context-aware AI pipelines, no code required.

CSV Enricher lets users define their business context once (through a MyProfile form) and reuse it across hundreds of CSV runs. The platform translates those visual forms into optimized LLM prompts — invisibly, correctly, and consistently.

A recruiter who needs to screen 500 resumes defines their ideal candidate profile once, uploads their CSV, and gets back a ranked list with match scores, reasoning, and next-step recommendations — in under 2 minutes.

Key Features
Reusable AI Templates

Industry-specific templates (Resume Screening, Lead Enrichment, Invoice Processing, Keyword Classification) that users can activate with one click.

MyProfile — Context Injection Engine

A visual form where users define their business criteria. That context is automatically injected into every prompt, making the AI behave as an expert in their specific domain.

Batch Processing Pipeline

Large CSVs are split into optimized batches and processed without overwhelming the OpenAI API. Rate limiting, retry logic, and progress tracking included.

Full Stripe Monetization

Subscriptions (Starter & Growth plans), credit-based usage billing, Stripe Checkout, and secure Webhook handling — fully integrated.

Technical Challenges & Solutions

Context Injection at Scale

Problem

Standard LLM wrappers produce generic results because they lack specific business logic. Users don't know how to write complex system prompts.

Solution

The "MyProfile" engine. It translates a visual form (business criteria) into a highly optimized, structured meta-prompt injected into every batch invisibly.

API Rate Limiting & Crashes

Problem

Sending a 5,000-row CSV directly to the OpenAI API causes immediate token limit errors, timeouts, and catastrophic failures.

Solution

A resilient batch-processing queue via FastAPI. It chunks the CSV dynamically, monitors OpenAI headers, applies slowapi backoff, and retries failed chunks automatically.
